SHIMLA

Perched in the Himalayas, Shimla is a charming hill station known for its colonial architecture, pine forests, and panoramic views. Once the summer capital of British India, it now welcomes travelers with its cool climate, vibrant bazaars, and scenic trails. Whether you're sipping chai on Mall Road or hiking to Jakhu Temple, Shimla offers a perfect blend of history, nature, and adventure.
