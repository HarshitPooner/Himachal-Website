KULLU MANALI

Surrounded by snow-capped peaks and lush valleys, Kullu Manali is a breathtaking destination in Himachal Pradesh. Known for its adventure sports, scenic beauty, and spiritual charm, it offers everything from river rafting in Kullu to paragliding in Solang Valley. Whether you're exploring Hadimba Temple, relaxing in Old Manali, or driving through Rohtang Pass, this twin town promises an unforgettable Himalayan escape.
