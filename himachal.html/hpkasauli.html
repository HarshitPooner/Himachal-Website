KASAULI

Tucked away in the Shivalik Hills, Kasauli is a peaceful colonial-era town known for its pine forests, misty trails, and charming architecture. With its serene ambiance, Christ Church, Gilbert Trail, and Monkey Point, Kasauli is perfect for those seeking a quiet retreat. Whether you're watching the sunset from Sunset Point or strolling through the Mall, Kasauli offers timeless tranquility.
