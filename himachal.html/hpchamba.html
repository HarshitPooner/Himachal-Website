CHAMBA

Nestled on the banks of the Ravi River, Chamba is a historic town in Himachal Pradesh known for its ancient temples, vibrant festivals, and scenic beauty. With its rich cultural heritage, the famous Bhuri Singh Museum, and the tranquil Chaugan meadow, Chamba offers a unique blend of spirituality and serenity. Whether you're exploring centuries-old architecture or enjoying the valley views, Chamba is a timeless Himalayan gem.
