Himachalli Food

Explore the rich flavors of the Himalayas

Dham

A festive platter of rice, dal, rajma, curd, and boor ki kari.

Siddu

Steamed wheat bread stuffed with spiced lentils, served with ghee and chutney.

Chana Madra

Chickpeas cooked in a yogurt-based gravy with aromatic spices.

Babru

Stuffed kachori made with black gram paste, deep-fried until crispy.

Aktori

A pancake made with buckwheat flour, popular in festive occasions.

Tudkiya Bhat

Spiced rice cooked with lentils, potatoes, and yogurt for a tangy flavor.

Mittha

A sweet dish made of rice, dry fruits, and sugar, served during celebrations.

Bhey

Lotus stem cooked with gram flour and spices, a crunchy delicacy.
